IronHold: Siege Attack

Defend your fortress, invade others, and rise to power.

Game Description

IronHold: Siege Attack is a solo tower defense experience where you play an active role in defending your stronghold. Join the fight alongside your automated defenses and crush waves of enemies. If you're defeated in battle, you’ll enter a temporary cooldown, but don’t worry — you will come back.

Earn money and powerful items by eliminating enemys. Use these rewards to enhance your stats, unlock new weapons, and prepare for even tougher battles.

Outside the battlefield, raid other strongholds to collect resources — even while offline. And for the brave, the “All Alone” mode strips away your defenses and leaves you fighting solo, but the risks come with much greater rewards.
